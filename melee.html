Super Smash Bros. Melee

Super Smash Bros. Melee is the second installment in the Super Smash Bros. Series. Super Smash Bros. Melee was release on the Nintendo Gamecube on November 21st, 2001 in Japan, and December 3rd, 2001 in North America, shortly after the GameCube's launch, and then on the following year in Europe and Australia on May 24th, 2002 and May 31st, 2002. The number of playable stages was increased and the cast of characters was increased to 25. The game also introduced single player event mode and adventure mode. In these modes if you completed specific tasks you could unlock characters. Super Smash Bros. Melee also popularized the Smash Bros. series competitive fighting scene due to the games increased game speed which can allow for interesting combo potential. Melee is also the GameCube's best selling game to date by a good margin.
