Super Smash Bros. Brawl

Super Smash Bros. Brawl is the third installment in the Super Smash Bros. Series. Super Smash Bros. Brawl was released on the Nintendo Wii January 31st, 2008 in Japan, March 9th, 2008 in North America, June 26th, 2008 in Australia, and June 27th, 2008 in Europe. Brawl was the first game to allow the player to play with different kinds of controllers. It was also the first game to feature characters that did not originate from Nintendo such as Snake, from Metal Gear, and the blue blurr, Sonic the hedgehog. The amount of playable characters was also increased from 25 to 35. It also introduced the stage builder feature, which allows the player the freedom to build their own stage to battle on. The game also greatly increased the number of stages and soundtrack that was featured.
